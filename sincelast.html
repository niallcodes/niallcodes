Questions

Fair asks

Is it really all free — no trial, no locked features?

Really. Unlimited monitors, every monitoring mode, every notification channel, full history and export — everything, for everyone. There is no paid tier to upsell you to.

Where does my data live?

On your device, full stop. Monitors, history, and snapshots are stored locally in your browser. Nothing you monitor is ever sent to us — there's no server to send it to. See the privacy policy.

How can it be unlimited and free?

Because the checks run in your browser, on your machine. There's no server bill for us to pass on to you, so there's no reason to cap you — and no reason to charge.

What's the catch? How is this sustainable?

No catch: there's nothing for us to run, so there's nothing for you to fund. Development is supported by optional Ko-fi donations from people who find it useful. No ads, no data selling — the policy is two minutes long.

Does it work while Chrome is closed?

Active monitors check on schedule while Chrome is open, and compare-on-visit catches everything else the moment you look. There's no cloud checking — that's exactly why it's private and free.
