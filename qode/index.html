A closer look

Built like an instrument.

Your brand, still scannable.

Colors, rounded modules and a center logo are easy — the hard part is knowing the result still scans. Qode's live indicator reacts to contrast, logo coverage and error-correction headroom as you design, and warns you in plain English before a code fails in the real world.

Nine types, one tool.

A WiFi code that joins the network on scan. A vCard that saves straight to contacts. Email, SMS, phone, location and calendar events — each with a purpose-built form and a live preview, assembled into the correct payload locally.

It reads codes, too.

Drop in an image, paste a screenshot, right-click any image on a page, or select a region of your screen. Qode decodes it on-device, detects what it is — link, WiFi, contact — and offers the right next step, including rebuilding it as your own code.

🔒

Private by architecture, not by promise.

Qode has no server and makes zero network requests — the QR engine and the decoder are bundled inside the extension. Whatever you encode or scan (URLs, WiFi passwords, contact details) is processed in your browser and never transmitted, to anyone, ever. No account, no analytics. Read the full privacy policy →
