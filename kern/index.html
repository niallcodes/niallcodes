A closer look

Built like an instrument.

See the whole type system.

One click scans everything a page loads: every family, its weights and usage, the total font payload in kilobytes — with problem flags for unused fonts, FOIT risk and family bloat. It's a performance review for typography.

Try any font, live.

Swap fonts on the real page — no mockups, no guessing. Pull from the Google Fonts library or upload your own file; uploads stay in the tab and vanish on reload. Drag variable axes and watch the page follow.

Export clean, real code.

Anything you inspect or collect exports as tidy CSS, Tailwind utilities and config, or design-token JSON — copy to clipboard or download. Built for handing straight to a codebase.

Perfect pairings, ready.

A curated pairing library covers 60+ popular families with vetted heading-and-body combinations — each previewable on the page you're looking at, loaded on demand from Google Fonts.

🔒

Private by architecture, not by promise.

Kern has no server. Everything runs locally in your browser: it collects nothing, transmits nothing, and needs no account. Uploaded try-on fonts never leave your machine. The only network request Kern ever makes is fetching a Google Font — and only when you ask it to. Read the full privacy policy →
