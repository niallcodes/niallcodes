Questions

The honest answers.

Does Focus Session track what I browse?
No. The extension never reads your browsing history or page content. It only knows the domains you've explicitly added to your blocklist. All session data stays in your browser's local storage — it never leaves your machine.
Can I use it on multiple computers?
Pro licenses activate on up to 2 devices. If you get a new computer, you can deactivate an old device from within the extension settings to free up a slot.
What if I want a refund?
Email hello@niallcodes.com within 30 days of purchase and we'll refund you, no questions asked. The free tier is permanent and doesn't require payment to stay useful, so we only expect people to upgrade when it genuinely adds value for them.
Does it work in incognito mode?
Not by default — Chrome disables extensions in incognito unless you explicitly enable them. Go to chrome://extensions → Focus Session → "Allow in incognito" if you want blocking to apply there too.
